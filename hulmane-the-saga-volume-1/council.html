The Grand Council of Bharatavarsha

When the signs of the Void Maw appeared in the heavens, the heroes of all 28 kingdoms were called. The Grand Council of Bharatavarsha is the unified command — one representative from each region, all 28 kingdom voices in the circle.

About the Council

The Grand Council of Bharatavarsha is the governing circle of Volume I. It convenes at the seat of Hulmane — a rotating assembly where each of the 28 kingdoms holds equal voice. Regional champions — the heroes who have attained the highest tattoo stage — represent their kingdoms in the Council chamber. The Council decides military strategy, treaty terms, and the rules of the Hulmane tournament itself.

The Council of Bharatavarsha is one circle within the larger Global Council — which will eventually seat representatives from all volumes (Europe, Asia, Americas, Australia, Africa). For now, the 28 kingdoms of Bharatavarsha are the first and the foundation.

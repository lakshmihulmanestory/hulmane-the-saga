## CENTRAL (2)

### Dandakaranya — Chhattisgarh
The Punished Forest. Buffalo | Hill Myna | Dandakarni (F) | Dandaka-Rakshasa (M) | Durga/Mahishasumardini | Bhima | Duryodhana+Bhima blend.
**Flaw seed:** Strength tied to wild identification; loses power when civilization demands action. → Ch 2 catalyst: tribal/urban schism.

### Hridaya-Sthana — Madhya Pradesh
The Heartland. Barasingha | Paradise Flycatcher | Dvadashashringa (M) | Adharma-Kendra (M) | Vishnu | Yudhishthira | Shakuni.
**Flaw seed:** Sees chess-like complexity in everything; paralyzed when urgent action needed. → Ch 2 catalyst: 12 micro-factions, each claiming dharma.

## EAST (4)

### Vajra-Bhumi — Bihar
The Thunderbolt Land. Gaur | House Sparrow | Vajramukha (M) | Tamasa (M) | Indra | Bhima | Duryodhana+Bhima blend.
**Flaw seed:** Refuses intelligence/strategy as weakness, brute force only. → Ch 2 catalyst: Nalanda intellectuals vs warrior-clans.

### Vana-Agni — Jharkhand
The Forest Fire. Indian Elephant | Asian Koel | Vanajara (M) | Vana-Raksha (M) | Aranya-Ganesha | Ekalavya | Dushasana.
**Flaw seed:** Believes education converts enemies; underestimates greed. → Ch 2 catalyst: mining barons vs forest custodians.

### Kalinga-Chakra — Odisha
The Wheel of Kalinga. Sambar Deer | Indian Roller | Jagannathi (F) | Chakra-Bheda (M) | Jagannath | Yudhishthira | Shakuni.
**Flaw seed:** Internal indecision; tries to honor every perspective. → Ch 2 catalyst: three Jagannath cults (Shabara tribal, Brahmin priestly, sea-merchant) declare independent rights.

### Sundara-Vana — West Bengal
The Beautiful Darkness. Fishing Cat | White-throated Kingfisher | Sundarvani (F) | Kala-Nadi (F, shadow-self) | Ganga | Draupadi | Kunti.
**Flaw seed:** Overprotectiveness of loved ones becomes a leverage point. → Ch 2 catalyst: family schism — sister-shadow Kala-Nadi turns her own house.

## NORTH (5)

### Kurukshetra-Bhoomi — Haryana
The Sacred Battleground. Blackbuck | Black Francolin | Kurukshetraa (F) | Kuru-Bhasma (M) | Dharma-raj | Yudhishthira | Ashwatthama.
**Flaw seed:** Idealism about dharma blinds her to costs; sacrifices innocents. → Ch 2 catalyst: caste-clan feud reignites; honor-killings recruit grief.

### Hima-Chhaya — Himachal Pradesh
The Snow Shadow. Snow Leopard | Western Tragopan | Himavati (F) | Hima-Asura (M, storm-trauma) | Durga | Draupadi | Duryodhana.
**Flaw seed:** Emotional isolation justified as strength. → Ch 2 catalyst: monasteries vs warrior-valleys; her isolation is exploited.

### Panch-Nada — Punjab
The Five-River Throne. Blackbuck | Shikra | Panchanada (M) | Panchavish (M) | Kartikeya | Arjuna | Karna.
**Flaw seed:** Seeks consensus; lacks ruthlessness. → Ch 2 catalyst: each river demands sovereignty; one river is literally poisoned.

### Ganga-Simhasana — Uttar Pradesh
The Throne on the Ganga. Barasingha | Sarus Crane | Gangaputra (M) | Adharma (M) | Krishna | Arjuna | Duryodhana+Shakuni blend.
**Flaw seed:** Split between personal destiny and kingdom duty. → Ch 2 catalyst: religious vs secular UP; Banaras underworld rises.

### Deva-Bhumi — Uttarakhand
The Land of Gods. Alpine Musk Deer | Himalayan Monal | Kasturika (F) | Hima-Mara (F) | Shiva-Kedarnath | Sahadeva | Jayadratha.
**Flaw seed:** Over-identified with masculine-protector role; rejects feminine healing. → Ch 2 catalyst: pilgrimage road becomes death-bureaucracy.

## NORTHEAST (8)

### Arunodaya-Kshetra — Arunachal Pradesh
The Land of the Dawn. Mithun | Great Hornbill | Udayagiri (M) | Asta-Asura (M) | Surya | Karna | Karna-shadow.
**Flaw seed:** Naive about trauma; assumes light heals everyone. → Ch 2 catalyst: dawn-tribes vs darkness-cultists who reject the sunrise.

### Ganda-Kshetra — Assam
The Rhinoceros Realm. One-Horned Rhino | White-winged Wood Duck | Kamalavarna (F) | Kama-Mara (F) | Kamakhya/Tantric Shakti | Bhima | Duryodhana.
**Flaw seed:** Afraid of her own wild tantric power. → Ch 2 catalyst: tantric covens splinter; suppressed initiates defect to Kama-Mara.

### Sangai-Nata — Manipur
The Dance of the Floating Deer. Sangai Deer | Mrs. Hume's Pheasant | Moirangthem (M) | Tamasa-Nrita (M) | Krishna | Arjuna | Ashwatthama.
**Flaw seed:** Perfectionism in form; loses rhythm under chaos. → Ch 2 catalyst: dance-clans split — "raw vs form" rebellion.

### Megha-Maata — Meghalaya
The Mother of Clouds. Clouded Leopard | Hill Myna | Meghanadi (F) | Megha-Daitya (M) | Indra | Kunti | Duryodhana.
**Flaw seed:** Maternal self-erasure. → Ch 2 catalyst: matrilineal Khasi vs incoming patrilineal claims; she is bypassed.

### Tlawmngaihna-Desh — Mizoram
The Land of Self-Sacrifice. Serow | Mrs. Hume's Pheasant | Tlangvala (M) | Parvata-Asura (M) | Shiva | Bheeshma | Duryodhana.
**Flaw seed:** Self-sacrifice creed is a vulnerability. → Ch 2 catalyst: youth reject self-sacrifice; mountain locks them in.

### Dzukou-Rashtra — Nagaland
The Valley Kingdom. Mithun | Blyth's Tragopan | Dzulevira (F) | Naga-Rakshasa (M) | Shakti | Draupadi | Ashwatthama.
**Flaw seed:** Torn between tribal/modern identity. → Ch 2 catalyst: 16 Naga tribes splinter, each claiming pure lineage.

### Beyul-Kshetra — Sikkim
The Hidden Sacred Land. Red Panda | Blood Pheasant | Kanchenjunga (M) | Krodha (M) | Avalokitesvara | Yudhishthira | Ashwatthama.
**Flaw seed:** Boundless compassion has no boundaries. → Ch 2 catalyst: refugee influx; mercy lets Krodha infiltrate the council.

### Tri-Pura — Tripura
The Triple Fortress. Phayre's Langur | Green Imperial Pigeon | Tripurari (M) | Tripura-Asura (M) | Tripura-deity | Buddhipriya | Invisible-sabotage parallel.
**Flaw seed:** Loves stones more than people. → Ch 2 catalyst: three cities openly war; people defect, stones crumble unnoticed.

## SOUTH (5)

### Chaya-Golkonda — Telangana
The Shadow Fortress. Spotted Deer | Indian Roller | Chittaranga (M) | Chitrabheda (M) | Kartikeya | Arjuna | Dushasana.
**Flaw seed:** Paranoia paralyzes even verified allies. → Ch 2 catalyst: city of mirrors fractures literally — thousand-face villain becomes the city itself.

### Dharmakshetra Amaravati — Andhra Pradesh
Krishnaveni (F) | Kalaniksha (F) | river-daughter | Blackbuck | Indian Roller | Ganga | Draupadi | Shakuni.
**Flaw seed:** Mercy toward enemy. → Ch 2 catalyst: Kalaniksha (spared in Ch 1) returns and weaponizes that mercy publicly.

### Parashurama-Kshetra — Kerala
The Axe-Born Land. Indian Elephant | Malabar Hornbill | Parashurama (M, 58 yrs) | Kala-Hasta (M) | Parashurama-avatar of Vishnu | Bheeshma | Karna.
**Flaw seed:** Honor-codes bind him; refuses dark techniques. → Ch 2 catalyst: 108 form-schools split; the 109th forbidden form is taught openly.

### Sangam Tamilakam — Tamil Nadu
The Eternal Assembly. Nilgiri Tahr | Emerald Dove | Kurinjiselvi (F) | Palinisi (F) | Parvati | Draupadi | Duryodhana.
**Flaw seed:** Patience bordering on passivity. → Ch 2 catalyst: Sangam assembly debates while Palinisi silences dissenters one by one.

### Vijayanagara Reborn — Karnataka *(pilot kingdom)*
The City of Victory. Indian Elephant | Indian Roller | Tungabhadra (F) | Bhoomi-Takshaka (F, serpent) | Indrani+Gajasena | Bhima | Ashwatthama.
**Flaw seed:** Haunted by imperial past; cannot choose between honoring predecessors and breaking violence-cycles. → Ch 2 catalyst: imperial revivalists vs reform progressives; Bhoomi-Takshaka returns as the "restorationist."

## WEST (4)

### Samudra-Dvipa — Goa
The Ocean's Pearl. Gaur | Black-crested Bulbul | Sagaradeva (M) | Maha-Vega (M, storm) | Varuna | Karna | Karna-shadow.
**Flaw seed:** Tradition-bound; cannot accept modern naval warfare. → Ch 2 catalyst: traditional fishermen vs port-corporation; foreign navies meddle.

### Simha-Dwara — Gujarat
The Lion's Gate. Asiatic Lion | Greater Flamingo | Simhavikrama (M) | Kala-Simha (M, kin-claimant) | Pride Council (seven ancestral lion-kings) | none unique | Duryodhana.
**Flaw seed:** Assumes legitimacy guarantees loyalty. → Ch 2 catalyst: Pride Council splinters; Kala-Simha calls a direct vote on succession.

### Swarajya Sahyadri — Maharashtra
The Self-Ruled Mountains. Indian Giant Squirrel | Yellow-footed Green Pigeon | Shivagati (F) | Vanaraksha (M, elder brother) | Hanuman | Abhimanyu | Duryodhana.
**Flaw seed:** Treats conflict as puzzle; lacks total commitment. → Ch 2 catalyst: 47 forts each declare independence; brother Vanaraksha gathers them under primogeniture claim.

### Maru-Maya — Rajasthan
The Desert of Illusions. Chinkara | Great Indian Bustard | Mrigatrishna (F) | Maru-Yama (M, death-fate) | Maya-Devi+Shakti | Shakuni | Ashwatthama.
**Flaw seed:** Overrelies on intuition; dismisses logic as cowardice. → Ch 2 catalyst: clans split over water-rights; Maru-Yama publishes a forged "fate-scroll" giving each clan a different prophesied destiny.

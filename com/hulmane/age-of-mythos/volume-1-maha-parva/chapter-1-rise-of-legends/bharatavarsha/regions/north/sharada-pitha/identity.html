The Villain — Avantivarman-Bhrasta

Name
Avantivarman-Bhrasta
Title
The Erasing Historian — He Who Owns What Is Remembered
Dark Power
Perfect mnemonic burning. Reads a manuscript once, remembers every word, then burns it. Becomes the sole keeper of every text he destroys.
Mirror Point
Both began as devoted students of the same tradition. One copies twice and gives one away. The other reads once and burns the original. Same love. Opposite custodianship.
Motivation
Believes Kashmir’s tragedy was incoherence — too many gods, too many languages, too many contradicting histories. He is curing the kingdom of its own confusion by editing the record to a single, clean narrative.

Culture & Heritage

The Villain — Kalapani-Asura

Name
Kalapani-Asura
Title
The Warden of Forgotten Cells — He Who Was Sentenced and Now Sentences
Dark Power
Locks any space — doors, ports, ship hulls, open water. Wherever he places his hand, isolation hardens — supplies cannot enter, ships cannot leave, signals die.
Mirror Point
Both have the same wound: an island used as a prison by every empire that ever ruled it. Onge-Nakshatra answers the wound by opening doors and trade routes. Kalapani-Asura answers it by hardening every lock the wound left behind. Same grievance. Opposite cure.
Motivation
Believes the islands were designed for isolation and that the mainland’s integration project is the disease, not the cure. Offers the tribes the gift his pain was given: complete separation from a world that wants them to change.

Culture & Heritage

Andamanese tribal traditions (Onge, Jarawa, Great Andamanese) — Nicobari shompen seafaring — colonial penal-colony memory of the Cellular Jail — the seven-spoke radial architecture of Port Blair — coconut-and-coir trade across the islands — Sentinelese sanctity (one tribe still uncontacted)

Sacred Tattoo System
Every hero carries a living record of their journey on their skin. The tattoo grows through four stages — Footprint, Leg, Half Body, Full Body — each earned through trials, not titles. When you see the mark, you know exactly where a warrior stands in their story.

Modern Power Drivers
In the new age, power is not won by swords alone. Five forces determine who controls the narrative: Economic leverage (oil, gas, food), Technology dominance (internet, satellites, AI), Soft power (music, media, social media), Geopolitical alliances, and People power (public sentiment, movements, narrative control).

Grand Council System
The Grand Council is the governing body of Bharatavarsha — one voice per region, 28 kingdoms represented in a single circle. Only heroes who have earned the Full Body tattoo may hold a seat. The Council decides military strategy, treaty terms, and the rules of the Hulmane tournament itself.

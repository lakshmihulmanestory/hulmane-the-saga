The Great Division

Origins of the Seven Continents  •  The Seven Brothers  •  Vimana-Loka

Before nations, before languages, before memory — there was one land. Eka-Bhumi: the single, breathing supercontinent covering the face of the young Earth. Then came the Seven. Enormous beings from a distant planet, crossing the void between stars not as conquerors, but as harvesters. What they sought lay buried beneath the land. What they left behind became everything — every continent, every tongue, every myth, every war.

Vimana-Loka — The Sky-Chariot World

A planet orbiting a dying star beyond the visible sky — home of the Seven Brothers, now depleted, now returning to reclaim what they left behind.

Origin

A planet of immense technological civilization, orbiting a star far beyond our solar system. Its people — the Vimana — mastered interstellar travel billions of years ago. They are not invaders in the human sense. They are miners, researchers, harvesters. Earth, to them, is a resource site — one they seeded with life as a side effect of their work.

The Seven Brothers

Seven elite Vimana were chosen for the Earth mission — one to govern each continental extraction zone. Each carries the elemental signature of his domain: sky, fire, wind, water, earth, void, and ice. They are enormous — towering above mountains — and older than most of Earth's geological formations.

Their Depletion

Whatever they extracted from Earth — Brahma-Dhatu, the cosmic metal — powered their entire civilization's energy. After billions of years of use, Vimana-Loka's reserves are nearly gone. The planet itself has begun to die. The Seven Brothers have no choice: they must return to Earth to claim the remaining 10% they left behind.

The Approach

Vimana-Loka has entered a gravitational path that brings it near our solar system once every several billion years. Astronomers have detected an anomalous object at the edge of observable range. Ancient texts across five civilizations describe this exact moment. The planet is not a metaphor. It is coming.

The Seven Brothers

Each brother claimed one continent as his domain. Each extracted his share of Brahma-Dhatu. Each left behind a script — and from that script, a language, a culture, a civilization grew.

From his Akasha-Lipi grew the root of all Indo-European languages. The Vedas were not written for humans — they were his research notes.

Physical Description

The Vimana appear humanoid — two legs, a torso, a head — but this is convergent evolution, not similarity to humans. They have six arms arranged in three pairs: the upper pair raised in what humans interpreted as a blessing gesture (it is actually a scanning array), the middle pair used for primary manipulation, and the lower pair partially concealed within their energy-robe field.

Their eyes emit a constant light across the full electromagnetic spectrum — including frequencies humans cannot see. Their "skin" is dark crystalline matter threaded with visible energy channels, like circuitry. When fully compressed to interact with a planetary surface, they stand between 9 and 15 kilometres tall. In deep space, they expand to sizes that dwarf planets.

The Crown — Seven Spires

Each Vimana's crown carries seven crystalline spires — one for each brother, each tuned to a different elemental frequency. These are not decorative. They are communication and monitoring arrays, keeping each brother connected to his brothers and to the Brahma-Dhatu extraction systems buried in each continent. The tip of each spire glows in the colour of the corresponding guardian: blue for Asia, red for Africa, pale blue for Europe, cyan for North America, green for South America, violet for Australia, and silver-white for Antarctica.

Size Comparison — Vimana vs Human

An adult Vimana stands as tall as the highest mountains on Earth. Their children — juveniles by Vimana standards — still tower over any human structure ever built.

Brahma-Dhatu — The Primordial Metal

Neither gold nor uranium nor any element known to science — a cosmic material formed only in the hearts of dying stars, buried in abundance beneath the young Earth four billion years ago.

What It Is

Brahma-Dhatu is a transuranium element — element 142 on an extended periodic table, existing in a metastable state impossible to synthesize but stable when extracted and processed through methods only the Vimana know. A single gram releases energy equivalent to twenty metric tons of uranium fission, cleanly, with zero radiation signature.

What the Brothers Did

The Seven Brothers spent what humans measure as three geological ages extracting Brahma-Dhatu from each continental deposit. They took 90%. They left 10% as structural — removing it would have destabilized Earth's tectonic system, collapsing the planet. That remaining 10% is what keeps Earth alive. It is also what the brothers are returning for.

The Akasha-Lipi — Sky Writing

In a desert in Rajasthan, beneath twenty meters of sand, explorers found a stone unlike any other — carved in a language no human had ever written. It took three thousand years of accumulated knowledge to decode. The translation changed everything.

The Discovery

Explorers digging in the Thar Desert in 2019 — searching for an underground water source — struck a smooth surface at 21 meters depth. What they uncovered took two years to fully excavate: a slab of unknown material, 40 meters long, 8 meters wide, impossibly smooth, covered in script unlike any human language ever recorded.

The material itself was Brahma-Dhatu alloy — explaining why it survived billions of years underground with zero degradation. And why decoding it required tools that hadn't existed until the 21st century.

Similar stones were found in 6 other locations worldwide — one per continent, each in the script of that region's guardian. The Rajasthan stone — Indra-Vara's stone — is the most complete, decoded at 68%. The others range from 2% to 41%.

"Each stone emits a constant low-frequency harmonic. Over millions of years, proto-human primates adapted their vocal systems to mimic that frequency. Language is not evolution. Language is mimicry of an alien script."
— Dr. A. Sharma, Akasha-Lipi Decoding Project, 2023

Timeline of the Earth — From One to Seven

Four billion years of history, compressed into the moments that matter.

🌍
~4 Billion Years Ago
Eka-Bhumi — The One Earth
Earth exists as a single supercontinent, geologically active, rich in Brahma-Dhatu distributed throughout its mantle. No complex life. No language. The metal waits.
🚀
~3.8 Billion Years Ago
The First Arrival — Vimana-Loka Approaches
The planet Vimana-Loka enters the outer solar system. Seven enormous beings descend in craft that dwarf mountains. They carry instruments beyond human understanding. They have done this before, elsewhere. Earth is not special — except in the quality of its Brahma-Dhatu deposit.
⚡
~3.8 – 3.2 Billion Years Ago
The Great Division
Each brother claims his territory. The continental division follows the exact distribution map of Brahma-Dhatu beneath the surface. The brothers use forces beyond geological scale to separate the landmass. This is what humans call the breakup of Pangaea, misattributed to tectonic drift alone.
⛏
~3.2 – 0.5 Billion Years Ago
The Long Extraction
Over billions of years, the seven brothers extract Brahma-Dhatu from their continental zones. As a byproduct — heat, chemical reactions, atmospheric alteration — life emerges. First microbial, then complex. The brothers observe but do not interfere significantly. Life is a curiosity, not their purpose.
📜
~500 Million Years Ago
The Akasha-Lipi — Scripts Left Behind
Before departing, each brother inscribes his record — what he extracted, what he left, how the extraction systems work, and a warning about removing the remaining Brahma-Dhatu. Each stone is written in Brahma-Dhatu alloy, guaranteed to survive until the brothers return.
🌌
~500 Million Years Ago
The Departure — And the Seeding of Language
The brothers leave Earth. In the process, proto-human primates begin adapting their vocal systems to mimic the resonance frequencies of the Akasha-Lipi stones, which emit a constant low-frequency signal. Over millions of years, this mimicry becomes language. Every human tongue is a biological echo of an alien script.
🏜
2019
The Rajasthan Discovery
Explorers in the Thar Desert excavate the first Akasha-Lipi stone — Indra-Vara's record for Asia. Six more are found on other continents in subsequent years. The stones emit a resonance that interferes with electronic equipment, explaining why they remained undetected for so long.
🔬
2019 – 2024
Decoding Begins — The World Unites
Teams across seven continents work simultaneously to decode their stones. As translations emerge, the picture clarifies: the brothers are coming back. The remaining 10% of Brahma-Dhatu is their target. And the Akasha-Lipi contains something else — partial instructions on how to use that 10% to resist Vimana-Loka itself.
⚔
Now — 2026 and Beyond
The Preparation — Volume I Begins in Bharatavarsha
Bharatavarsha — the Indian subcontinent, territory of Indra-Vara — holds the most decoded stone and the highest concentration of remaining Brahma-Dhatu. It is ground zero. The kingdoms of India must unite not just politically but dharmically, generating a force that the stone's partial instructions describe as the one thing the brothers fear: organized, unified resistance at the source.

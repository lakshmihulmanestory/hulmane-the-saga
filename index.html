MAHAHULMANE

The Great Assembly of Warriors

Inspired by the Mahabharata, MahaHulmane is a tale where courage and faith speak louder than swords. Even when darkness gathers behind the stars — or rises from within the kingdom itself — the people, their trust, and their Dharma will stand against it all. Dharma is the wind that carries every hero forward. All the art of this world begins and ends with courage.

The Heroes of MahaHulmane

Twenty-eight kingdoms. Twenty-eight heroes. Each carries their own sacred symbol, their own tattoo, their own story — so when you see them, you know exactly who they are and where they come from. This is the world at a glance: warriors, guardians, legends, all rising together against the gathering dark.

Heroes from every kingdom, each carrying the mark of their sacred beast — united against the darkness.

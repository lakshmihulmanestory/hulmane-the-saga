# Team Structure & Responsibilities

Since this project is primarily architected on weekends, the team structure must be highly **Modular**. The world is broken down into specific "Domains" to prevent writers and contributors from clashing or overlapping.

## A. The Core Roles

1. **The Lore Master (You)**
   * **Responsibility**: Own the "World Saga".
   * **Duties**: Approve all major plot points, character deaths, and the fundamental "Laws of Physics/Magic" in the world. You are the ultimate authority on canon.

2. **Lead Narrative Architect**
   * **Responsibility**: Manage the "Main Thread" (the overarching saga).
   * **Duties**: Ensure the overarching narrative maintains momentum. They string together the major milestones of the main protagonists.

3. **Regional Writers**
   * **Responsibility**: Own a specific "Kingdom" or "Volume".
   * **Duties**: Write the side quests, local lore, character development, and cultural details within the boundaries set by the Lore Master and Narrative Architect.

4. **Editor / Continuity Checker**
   * **Responsibility**: Maintain consistency for a "Mega Story."
   * **Duties**: Ensure continuity across volumes (e.g., ensuring a character in Volume 1 doesn't inexplicably have a different eye color or weapon in Volume 5). They audit the World Saga.

## B. How to Assign Work

### 1. The "Prompt" System
Do not just say "write a story." Give the writers a structured **Story Brief**.

*Example of a concise brief:*
* **Setting**: The Kingdom of [X].
* **Mandatory Event**: Two brothers must argue over a magic artifact.
* **Outcome**: The artifact must be broken.

*(See `5_story_brief_template.md` for the template).*

### 2. The "Wiki" Method
Use a structured documentation approach (via Markdown, Notion, or Obsidian).
* **Rule**: Every time a writer creates a new town, creature, or character, they **must** create a corresponding "Wiki entry" for it.
* **Benefit**: Other team members can easily reference this lore, preventing duplication and enriching the interconnected feel of the world.
